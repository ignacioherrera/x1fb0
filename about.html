ABOUT US

At GMSTEK, we believe that the effective interplay between design and functionality is what sets our websites, web shops and apps apart from other digital agencies. We’re transparent with our customers and because we consider their projects to be our own, the results are beautiful, useful and responsive.

Rooted in a European work ethic and proudly established in Dubai, United Arab Emirates, all of our work is done from our gorgeous studio in the heart of Business Bay. This allows us greater control over our output, as well as the ability to create a true family atmosphere with our staff and clients. Our door is always open for a good cup of coffee and conversation.

James Wiley

Chief Executive Officer

Hello! We are the people behind X-1 and we’re on a mission

In an over 24-year career in the FBO business, Mr. Wiley has served in multiple operating positions including Line Service Technician, Line Service Manager, Flight School Manager, Ground Services Manager, Operations Manager Transition Manager (for acquisitions), Assistant General Manager and General Manager. He began his career at Priester Aviation and subsequently served at DB Aviation, Mercury Air Center, Million Air, Sheltair and Landmark Aviation where he was the General Manager of their Opa Locka FBO. Wherever he has been, he has improved operations, resulting in increased margins and profitability. Mr. Wiley is a licensed pilot. He holds a B.S. in Aerospace Science from the State University of New York.

Stephen Sandler

Chief Financial Officer

Mr. Sandler has over 25 years of experience in providing corporate strategy, marketing communications and strategy, and financial services for a wide range of companies. Prior to GMSTEK, Mr. Sandler was the founder and president of SRK Group, and founder and president of VMARK Associates. Both were leading marketing strategy and communications firm serving the life sciences industry and other clients. Products for which Mr. Sandler and his firms provided services have generated revenue in the billions of dollars.

Clients included Boston Scientific (18 years), Johnson & Johnson, Smith & Nephew, as well as many early stage companies. Earlier in his career, Mr. Sandler served as the CFO of Research Media, Inc. a company that developed product launch and educational programs for pharmaceutical firms. Prior to that, he was the CFO of National Information Services, a publicly held minicomputer systems company. Mr. Sandler began his career with Peat Marwick, Mitchell & Company (now KPMG) where he passed the CPA examination. He is a graduate of the University of Pennsylvania.

Humberto Zuluaga

Director of IT Infrastructure

Humberto is an IT Professional with over 20 years of hands-on IT Management for companies like Mondelez, Kraft Foods and Cadbury Schweppes. His background includes the design and implementation of cloud based solutions using market leading platforms like AWS (achieved several important certifications), and the implementation and management of Infrastructure Services like Network, Hosting, Telephony, End User Services and Help Desk for big scale companies with thousands of users. Working a big part of his professional career on the customer side has given him a clear understanding of the customer´s needs and the type of partnering that must exist between a technology company and its customers.
